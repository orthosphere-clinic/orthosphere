About Dr. Tushar Gupta

Dr. Tushar Gupta is a distinguished Orthopaedic Surgeon with a rare triple-crown qualification from India's premier institutes (MAMC & AIIMS). With specialized training from Germany and the UK, he brings world-class expertise in Joint Replacement and Arthroscopy to New Delhi.

Premier Education

Alumnus of MAMC (MBBS) and AIIMS (MS & M.Ch). Secured AIR 1 in the M.Ch National Entrance Examination 2022 and AIR 95 in pre-medical exams.

Global Expertise

Fellowship in Revision Arthroplasty at ENDO-Klinik, Germany. Visiting Surgeon at Wrightington NHS Hospital, United Kingdom.

Advanced Techniques

Expert in Robotic Joint Replacement and Direct Anterior Approach (DAA) for Hip. A.O Trauma & Joint Reconstruction certified (Switzerland).

Clinical Excellence
