About Dr. Gupta

Excellence in Orthopaedic Surgery

Specialties

Comprehensive Orthopaedic Services

From joint replacements to sports injuries, receive personalized care tailored to your unique needs.

Hip Replacement

Total & partial hip replacements using the latest implants for long-lasting results and quicker recovery.

Knee Replacement

Expertise in total & unicompartmental knee replacements with robotic-assisted precision.

Knee Arthroscopy

Minimally invasive keyhole surgery for ligament tears, meniscus injuries, and cartilage damage.

Shoulder Surgery

Sports Injuries

ACL reconstruction, meniscal repair & comprehensive treatment for athletic injuries.
